Cognizant is an American multinational IT services and consulting company that helps other businesses modernize their technology and processes. Founded in 1994, 
it provides a wide range of services, including cloud solutions, AI, digital engineering, and business process outsourcing, across various industries like banking,
 healthcare, and retail. The company, which is headquartered in Teaneck, New Jersey, has a large global workforce, with significant employee bases in India. 

Zensar is an Indian digital solutions and technology services company, headquartered in Pune and part of the RPG group. 
It provides services across application, infrastructure, and cloud management, with a focus on areas like digital engineering, data analytics, and AI. 
The company serves various industries, including banking, financial services, insurance, manufacturing, and consumer services, and has a global presence with offices in India, the US, Europe, and Africa. 

Key reasons to mention
Alignment with company values and mission: 
Show that you believe in what the company does and that your own values align with theirs, for example, if they are committed to innovation, sustainability, or a specific social cause. 
Growth and learning opportunities: 
Mention your desire to learn new skills and how the company's focus on professional development, training programs, or career advancement will help you grow in your field. 
Contribution to the company: 
Explain what you can bring to the team. Connect your specific skills, past accomplishments, or expertise to the requirements of the job and the company's goals. 
Company's reputation and impact:
 Point to the company's strong reputation, innovative products, or the significant impact it has made in the industry. 
Culture and team environment: 
If you've heard great things about the work environment, mention that you value a collaborative and supportive culture and believe you would fit in well.
